Important Notes & Tips

Usage Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and demonstration purposes only. It should not be used for making critical agricultural decisions without consulting a qualified professional.

Model & Data Insights

Our AI is trained for high accuracy on specific datasets like PlantVillage. For best results, use clear, well-lit images focusing on a single leaf against a simple background.

Image Quality Tips

Ensure the plant leaf is the main subject, well-focused, and fills a good portion of the image. Avoid blurry photos, images with complex backgrounds, or multiple leaves if possible.
